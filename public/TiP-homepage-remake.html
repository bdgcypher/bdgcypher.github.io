What is Tip?

TiP stands for ‘Transparency in Politics.’ At TiP, we do not believe that an annual vote is enough power for the people. Our goal is to empower voters by providing them with a platform to amplify their voices on their own schedule.

ASPIRING ACTIVIST?

Activism has never been easier.

TiP lowers the barrier to entry for political involvement. We provide an easy to use platform that allows you to send “TiPs” directly to YOUR representatives in seconds; forget about missing town hall meetings and cold calling elected offices.

COMMUNITY LEADER?

We are in this together.

Are you part of a community group, local organization or government? Thank you for your work on the behalf of the people; we are here to help. Mobilize your base to TiP and raise your collective voice while making an impact.

ELECTED OFFICIAL?

Introducing local governments to the modern era.

We work with elected officials, governments, and political consultants to keep a pulse on public perception. You can use TiP to survey citizens, live-track their responses, and engage directly with those who align with your platform.
